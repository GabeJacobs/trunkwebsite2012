Trunklove, on the town.

We wear our Trunkiness on our sleeves, sure, but after the show or – gasp – after graduation, how can you really make sure the world knows your proper last name is ‘Trunker’? Order a new round of Trunk sweatshirts, that’s how. With Ed Rossel De Jongh Trunker’s fantastic seal design, created at the same time he redrew our tees (in 2005). Read on!

 

Choices, choices. You can choose from two American Apparel sweatshirts: the Flex Fleece Zip Hoodie, and the California Fleece Pullover. Only some of AA’s colors are available here; all of them look different than you’d expect. Click-through to pick the color and style that suits your fancy. Go to a store, even. Be con-fritters in your decision!

Design. The seal looks like this, will be eight inches across and printed in white ink on the sweatshirt of your choice. On pullover sweatshirts, the artwork will be centered on the chest. On zippered sweatshirts, by popular acclamation, it will be on the back.

Ordering & delivery. You can place your order any time before February 10th. Do it now, though, because you’re gonna forget. Shipping and handling is included in the sticker price. Expect your hoodie 4-5 weeks after the deadline, with updates from me along the way. Unfortunately they will not be ready in time for reunion!
