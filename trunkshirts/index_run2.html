The Union T-Shirt Project
 

Church picnic? Dance party? You’re ready.

The tees were a huge hit last spring; we’re back with better ink, original artwork, and the soft fabric you love so much. In partnership with Student Senate and the Class of 2011, we’re offering a bold new shirt that lets you wear your seminarian pride on your sleeve (as it were) and an improved run of the beloved seal on classic gray. All shirts are only $11 and supply is limited, so hurry up! Look for us in the Pit during lunch all week.

 

The Seal Comes on American Apparel’s Tri-Blend shirt, in both Women’s & Unisex cuts. Larger shirts use a new, larger printing of the seal.

Summer Scrawl Comes on AA’s soft 50/50 shirt. Unisex shirts come in red, a limited supply of Women’s cut (& only the Women’s) come in black.

All shirts were printed in Brooklyn by the fantastic Kingsland Printing using water-based ink – none of last year’s plasticky stuff! Sizing charts: U/W
