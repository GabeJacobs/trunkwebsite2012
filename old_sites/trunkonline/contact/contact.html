booking

Trunk would love to come perform for your day-care center/elementary school/children's hospital/public library/other similar place! We will bring a rag-tag group of Trunkers, a couple bags of colorful costumes, and boundless enthusiasm for making your kids smile and laugh!

Meet Ali. She's here to guide you through the astoundingly simple process (on your end, at least) of booking Trunk for a show - and all we ask of you is a small donation to charity if you have the budget for it! The first thing you need to do is send her an email to let her know you're interested and when is best for you this spring.

 

our charity

In light of Hurrican Katrina, Trunk has deviated from the standard focus on small, local, child-orientated charities to choose a small, child-orientated effort that will make an impact somewhere else: for the youth of Lousiana and Mississippi. The University of Souther Mississippi's "10,000 Books" relief effort will bring - you guessed it - 10,000 chidlren's books to 10 Gulf Coast area libraries ravaged by the storm. USM has secured matching programs from publishing companies to make each donation doubly effective. Due to the nature of this charity (a small group), we're asking that all checks be made out to the Traveling Treasure Trunk; we'll collect everything and donate one lump sum at the end of the semester.
