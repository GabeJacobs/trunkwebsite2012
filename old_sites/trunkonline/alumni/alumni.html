trunk-net

Trunk-net is Trunk's very own Yahoo! group for Trunkers past and present. We use it to tell everyone about new additions to the troupe, on-campus shows, and the odd silly story or bit of networking. There are currently over 60 members, and we'd love to add you to the list!

Just follow this external link to the main page and sign on up!
