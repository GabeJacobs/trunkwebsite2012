14 September 2005 :: Come one, come all!

For those of you who haven't noticed the plates all over campus, we here at Trunk are putting out our semiannual call for all Tufts students to traipse on down to the Performance Hangar for the succintly enjoyable bit of game-playing known as our auditions! Held at the beginning of each semester, the Fall '05 try-outs are this Saturday, September 17th. We apoligize for the belatedness of this announcement, but be sure to run down to the Aidekman call board to sign on up for a slot on Saturday (call backs Sunday). If all the spots are filled, call Jess at 973.650.5760 or just show up - we love walk-ins.

6 July 2005 :: ArrrtBeat, mateys!

Somerville's renowned and beloved ArtBeat is (thankfully) back for another stellar year, and Trunk is proud to be performing as we have in recent years. Leah Trunker reports, "we're scheduled to perform at 1 PM and 3 PM," so though we're not yet up on the websifete, be sure to come out to support the Somerville Arts Council and keep an eye out for some Trunkers - further details are sure to develop on the ArtBeat website. 4 May 2005 :: Alive and kickin'!

Consider this the official, "Welcome to TrunkOnline!" message. The site, after manymanymany hours in Tufts' beautiful Eaton Computer Lab, is up. It's up, and I like to think it's pretty. Watch out for some broken links while perusing our old scrapbooks (clicking "TRUNK!" won't take you back to this page, like it will from everywhere else). I also wouldn't mind some more content on the photo and alumni pages, but there are finals to study for, you know? You know.

I'd imagine that there will be few updates in the coming months, as I (your illustrious webmaster) head home, without access to all this wonderful technology. So sit tight. Trunk will be performing at Somerville's ArtBeat in Davis Square in July, but aside from that, we will all be job-searching (for the grads) and relaxing for a few months. It's all in preparation for another wonderful fall season. Stay tuned.

We'll be back another while, but now... it's time to go!
